## How to use these videos

1. **First pass - Microsoft Learn / study cram** (one sitting).
2. **Second pass** - focused channel deep-dives for any topic where you didn't feel confident.
3. **Third pass** - Worklab + Responsible AI for the *why* behind each principle.
4. Then take the **Practice Assessment** (see the sidebar).
