## Readiness checklist

- [ ] You can name the **3 domains** + their weights.
- [ ] You can list the **6 responsible AI principles**.
- [ ] You can explain the difference between **Microsoft Copilot (free)** and **Microsoft 365 Copilot**.
- [ ] You can recite **GCSE** (Goal, Context, Source, Expectations).
- [ ] You can pick the right Copilot surface for: long email thread, missed meeting, doc to deck, spreadsheet trends, drafting an executive memo, public-news search.
- [ ] You can explain **what a hallucination is** and **3 ways to mitigate**.
- [ ] You can describe how **sensitivity labels** flow through M365 Copilot output.
- [ ] You can list **5 prompt anti-patterns** + their fixes.
- [ ] You can explain why Copilot in Excel needs a structured **table**.
- [ ] You can explain why **regulated data should not go into free web Copilot**.
