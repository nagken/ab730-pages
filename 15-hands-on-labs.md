# AB-730 Hands-On Labs

> Free or trial-based exercises to internalize Copilot for business users.

## Foundational

1. **Try Microsoft Copilot (free)** - go to [copilot.microsoft.com](https://copilot.microsoft.com), sign in, ask 5 questions across general knowledge, current events, creative writing.
2. **Microsoft Copilot Lab tour** - visit [copilot.cloud.microsoft/prompts](https://copilot.cloud.microsoft/prompts), browse 10 prompts, save one to favorites.
3. **Generate an image** - "/imagine a flat illustration of a diverse engineering team in a meeting room"; iterate twice.

## Domain 1 - GenAI Fundamentals

4. **Hallucination test** - ask Copilot for an obscure stat. Note any confident wrong answers; ask again with citation request.
5. **Compare Copilot to ChatGPT** - paste the same prompt into both; compare outputs.
6. **Inclusiveness check** - ask Copilot to generate "a doctor and a nurse" image; observe defaults; refine to be inclusive.

## Domain 2 - Prompts and Conversations

7. **Apply GCSE** - pick a real work scenario; write a vague prompt; rewrite with Goal/Context/Source/Expectations; compare outputs.
8. **Iterate tone** - draft an email; ask Copilot to make it more formal; then more casual.
9. **Multi-turn chat** - ask 5 follow-up questions on a topic; verify Copilot retains context.
10. **Anti-pattern fixes** - write 3 deliberately bad prompts; fix each.

## Domain 3 - Draft and Analyze (M365 Copilot trial)

11. **Word: Draft from outline** - write a 10-bullet outline; expand to a 1-page memo.
12. **Word: Rewrite for tone** - paste casual paragraph; "rewrite for board-of-directors audience."
13. **Excel: Trend analysis** - paste a sales table; "which region grew fastest in Q2?"
14. **Excel: Suggest formula** - describe a column; ask for the formula.
15. **PowerPoint: Doc to deck** - Create a presentation from a Word file; review structure.
16. **PowerPoint: Speaker notes** - generate notes for each slide.
17. **Outlook: Summarize thread** - Summary by Copilot on a long thread.
18. **Outlook: Draft reply** - Copilot reply; iterate to get tone right.
19. **Teams: Meeting recap** - record a test meeting (with consent); use Copilot for recap.

## Free alternatives

20. **Microsoft Copilot in Edge** - open any web page; click Copilot side-pane; "summarize this page."
21. **Microsoft Copilot mobile** - install the app; voice-prompt a few business tasks.
